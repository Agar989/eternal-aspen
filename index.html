Eternal Aspen

"Love is the shadow that ripens the wine of our existence."

From ancient texts, we learn of a man
Who laid down his life for love—
A love that embraced all of mankind.
Sacrifice, the purest testament,
Where brave souls surrender their breath
For ideals too vast to grasp.

But how far can my love stretch
In this fragile reality we inhabit?
To claim that my desire is eternal
Would be a falsehood,
For in this perilous world,
I would offer my heart,
A shield against the chaos
To protect you, my dear.

I may weave chaos into my existence,
Yet I yearn to guard you,
To stand as your sentinel,
Though miles may lie between us.
Am I your hero?
Not in the grand tales of valor,
But a humble protector,
Suspended in the space of longing,
My thoughts tethered to you,
My sweet Aspen.

In this vast expanse,
I catch glimpses of your beauty,
A vision that ignites my spirit.
Words falter when I feel undone,
Yet in the silence,
One name echoes through the void—
My last breath, my final whisper:
Aspen.

Forever yours,
Anthony
